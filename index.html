Bio

On the fifth day of the first month in the year 1992. Everything seemed in place. All was right and people believed in something, someone.

On the fifth day of the fifth month in the year 2021. Nothing seemed as it seemed. Edgar Morris picked up his bags, went out the door and never looked back nor ahead.

On the seventh day of the seventh month in the year 2021. Everything is as it is ought to be, only to be regarded as one step closer to another step. Edgar Morris dropped down on his knees, looked up to the marble stone in front and asked for approval.

The wind was howling and the rain kept falling on the day the band formerly known as ccexpex was renamed for marketing reasons into the purely fictional otherworldy figure of Edgar Morris. Prior to ccecpex, Edgar spent his younger years playing with various bands the likes of Lovin' Whiskey, Odilon, Houston's Problem, Witter and Rufus. Elco. After a few years in the gutter, it is time to pick up where the ship has stranded. Set of and see where this musical journey takes you. For like Bilbo said. I'd like to see mountains again. Mountians Gandalf! This year all the energy was spent building a new persona. a new mask to put on, a new smile to layer on top of hte old ones. New goals, actions, failures and doubts. Only to find what was never ment to be found and to lose, what never thought was in your possession.
